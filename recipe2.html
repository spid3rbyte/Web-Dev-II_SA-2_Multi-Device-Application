Pecan Crusted Honey-Mustard Salmon



    Ingredients:

    Coconut oil,
    1/4 cup Dijon mustard,
    3 tbsp Honey,
    1 1/2 tbsp Bread crumbs,
    1/4 cup Pecans,
    28.35 grams Atlantic salmon,
    4 fillet Salt,
    1 tsp Pepper,
    1 tsp Parsley,
    4 tsp


How To Prepare:

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
2. In a small bowl, mix melted coconut oil, mustard, and honey.
3. In another small bowl, mix bread crumbs, pecans, and parsley.
4. Place salmon filets on a foil covered baking sheet. Season salmon with salt and pepper and pour the honey mixture over the tops.
Sprinkle with the bread crumb mixture.
5. Bake salmon until it flakes easily with a fork, about 20 minutes. Enjoy!





Parmesan Chicken Cutlets



    Ingredients:

    Chicken breast,
    8 breast fillet Wheat flour,
    1 cup Salt,
    1 tsp Pepper,
    1/4 tsp Egg,
    2 extra large Water,
    2 tbsp Bread crumbs,
    1 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese,
    1/2 cup Butter,
    2 tbsp Olive oil,
    2 tbsp


How To Prepare:

1. Pound or butterfly the chicken breasts until they are an even thickness (1/4-inch thickness works best).
2. Set up three dinner plates or shallow bowls for the coating breading process. On the first, combine flour, salt, and pepper together.
On the second, beat the eggs and water.
On the third, combine the bread crumbs and parmesan cheese.
3. Coat the chicken breasts on both sides in the flour mixture. Then dip the chicken (both sides again) in the egg mixture.
Finally, dredge both sides of the chicken in the breadcrumb mixture until completely coated.
4. In a frying pan, over medium-high heat melt one tablespoon of butter and one tablespoon of olive oil and cook 2 of the chicken breasts on medium heat until cooked through (2-3 minutes on each side for a thinly pounded breast; 3-4 minutes on each side if you've left the breasts thicker.)
Cook the remaining breasts using the remaining butter and olive oil.





Tuscan Tortellini Salad



    Ingredients:

    Tortellini,
    432 grams Olive oil,
    8 tbsp Balsamic vinegar,
    2 tbsp Salt,
    1 dash Pepper,
    1 dash Prosciutto Cubetti,
    24 oz Spinach,
    3 cup Organic Sundried Tomatoes,
    170 grams Parmesan cheese,
    8 tbsp


How To Prepare:

1. In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook tortellini according to package directions.
Drain and transfer to large serving bowl.
2. Make dressing: Whisk together olive oil and balsamic vinegar and season with salt and pepper.
3. To bowl, add prosciutto, spinach, sun-dried tomatoes, and dressing and toss until well combined.
4. Garnish with parmesan.
Enjoy!





Fruit Parfait



    Ingredients:

    Banana,
    2 medium Strawberries,
    2 cup, halves Greek yogurt,
    16 oz Blueberries,
    2 cup Granola,
    1 cup


How To Prepare:

1. Slice banana and quarter strawberries and put them in a bowl.
2. Add yogurt then blueberries.
3. Top with granola when ready to eat.
Enjoy!





Grilled Salmon Burger Patties



    Ingredients:

    Atlantic salmon,
    80 oz Onions,
    1 1/4 cup, chopped Dijon mustard,
    33/100 cup Lemon juice,
    3/4 cup Salt,
    2 tbsp Parsley,
    4 tbsp Dill,
    2 tbsp Bread crumbs,
    2 cup


How To Prepare:

1. With a food processor or blender use the "pulse" option to break up the salmon until it is in chunks.
2. Add all of the other ingredients to your salmon chunks.
3. Mix together well, and form 5oz burger patties.
4. Grill at 400 degrees F until well cooked (8-10 minutes).
